History

21.7.2014

Humble Beginnings

We first started baking pies in our converted utility room in our house. We sold our pies at West Dorset Farmers Markets.

28.7.2015

Windsbatch Farm

The bakery at Windsbatch Farm, in the old dairy enabled us to grow the business and venture into wholesale to Farm Shops as well as Farmers Markets.

03.3.2017

New Beginnings

Our move to New Street Bakery has given us the opportunity to diversify and to offer traditional artisan baking as well as the pie range. Tommy our Baker is very talented. We look forward to serving our customers in Dorchester and further afield.
